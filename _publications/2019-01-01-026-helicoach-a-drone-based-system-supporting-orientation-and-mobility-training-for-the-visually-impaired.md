abstract: >-
  Orientation and mobility (O&M) training is essential for improving the independence and wellbeing of
  the visually impaired. However, the shortage of qualified trainers and the unengaging training
  contents limit the number of O&M training recipients. In this paper, we propose a drone-based
  intelligent tutor system - HeliCoach - to provide cost-effective and personalized O&M training. We
  first elaborate on the system design and potential scenarios of HeliCoach use. We then demonstrate
  the effectiveness of this concept using a preliminary user study. Finally, we discuss the
  implication and challenges of this system.
