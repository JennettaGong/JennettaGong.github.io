abstract: >-
  We demonstrate a desktop robot OYaYa that imitates users’ emotional facial expressions and helps
  users manage emotions. Multiple equipped sensors in OYaYa enable multimodal interaction; for
  example, it recognizes users’ emotions from facial expressions and speeches. Besides, a dashboard
  illustrates how users interact with OYaYa and how their emotions change. We expect that OYaYa allows
  users to manage their emotions in a fun way.
