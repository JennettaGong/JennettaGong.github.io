<section class="atlas-panel"><div data-lang="en"><h2>About</h2><p>Jiangtao Gong is an Associate Professor at the School of Design (Interdisciplinary), Shanghai Jiao Tong University. She received her Ph.D. from the Future Laboratory of Tsinghua University under Professor Yingqing Xu, and completed postdoctoral research with Dr. Yong Rui and Dr. Qianying Wang at Lenovo.</p><p>Previously, she worked in the Robotics Laboratory at Tsinghua University's Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR), where she led the Human–Computer Interaction Research Group (AIR-HCI).</p></div><div data-lang="zh"><h2>个人简介</h2><p>龚江涛现任上海交通大学设计学院（交叉学科）副教授。她于清华大学未来实验室获得博士学位，师从徐迎庆教授；随后在联想集团与芮勇博士、王茜莺博士开展博士后研究。</p><p>此前，她曾就职于清华大学智能产业研究院（AIR）机器人实验室，并担任人机交互研究组（AIR-HCI）负责人。</p></div></section>
